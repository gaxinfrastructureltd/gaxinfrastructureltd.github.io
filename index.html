About Us

GAX Infrastructure Ltd is a dynamic civil engineering firm dedicated to delivering innovative, sustainable infrastructure solutions across Nigeria. With headquarters in Abuja and an office in Enugu, we provide nationwide delivery.

Our mission is to build strong, lasting foundations that empower communities and shape a brighter future through top-quality engineering and construction services.

  • Road and Bridge Construction
  • Residential and Commercial Building
  • Land Surveying and Site Analysis
  • Concrete and Asphalt Paving
  • Project Management and Consultancy
  • Nationwide Delivery and Support
